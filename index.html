An Overview of Lentils

What are lentils?

Lentils are a gift from nature. A highly nutritious legume enjoyed by many cultures all over the world.

Why are they good?

Lentils are a small but mighty legume. They are supremely healthy, tasty, and sustainable! Lentils provide essential nutrients needed for a healthy diet. As the meat industry tortures animals and harms the humans who process meat, an alternative food source is needed for a healthy, happy soceity. Lentils are the answer! They provide all the nutrients that are typically obtained through animal products. That's why humans have been enjoying lentils since before pottery was invented!

Lentils do not require much water, and they are nitrogen-fixing, that means they dont need as much fertilizer; this is hugely beneficial to the environment! 45% of the World's lentils are grown in Canada, the relatively infertile land there can handle this, as lentils are not a picky crop.
